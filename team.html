Mary Naber
Founder & President

Mary Naber is the driving force behind Sage Stone Wealth Management and one of the country's foremost authorities on ethical investing. With over two decades of experience managing wealth for high-net-worth clients, she has built her practice on a simple belief: you should never have to choose between your values and your financial security.

Mary was the first researcher to publish academic work integrating faith and investing in the Journal of Investing, a landmark contribution that helped define the field of values-aligned portfolio management as it exists today.

Prior to founding Sage Stone, Mary served as Senior Vice President of Investments at Merrill Lynch's Beverly Hills office. She is a nationally renowned speaker, and her work has been referenced in multiple books and publications.

Michael Lopez
Vice President

Michael Lopez brings a rare combination of global financial expertise and deep analytical rigor to Sage Stone's client work. Trained at Harvard and the University of Melbourne, he specializes in translating complex macroeconomic realities into clear, actionable investment strategies.

Michael's career spans senior roles across financial advisory, analytics, and accounting. He served as a Financial Management Consultant to Charter Mason in Melbourne, a Senior Financial Analyst at Kaiser Permanente, and held roles at Arthur Andersen. He also served as a Financial Advisor at Merrill Lynch in the San Francisco area.
